# EcoTrack AI 🌱

A Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact through personalized insights and sustainable lifestyle recommendations.

## Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the biggest challenges facing our world today. Many people want to live more sustainably but lack awareness of how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions.

EcoTrack AI helps users:

* Calculate their carbon footprint
* Understand the environmental impact of daily habits
* Receive personalized sustainability recommendations
* Encourage eco-friendly decision making

## Features

### Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculate estimated carbon emissions based on:

* Transportation habits
* Energy consumption
* Lifestyle choices
* Daily activities

### Sustainability Dashboard

Visualize environmental impact through:

* Carbon footprint metrics
* Interactive data visualization
* Environmental awareness indicators

### Personalized Recommendations

Receive actionable suggestions to:

* Reduce carbon emissions
* Improve sustainability habits
* Make environmentally conscious choices
